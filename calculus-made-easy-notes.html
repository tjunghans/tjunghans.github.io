What is a function?

  • Functions are the most fundamental concept in mathematics
  • Term was first used in 1673 by Gottfried WIlhelm Leibniz
  • In traditional calculus, a function is defined as a relationship between two terms (variables)
  • The two terms are called variables because their values vary
  • The two terms are usually named x and y
  • For every x there is one value of y
  • y is a function of x
  • x is the indepentend variable
  • y is the dependent variable because its value depends on x
  • letters at the end of the alphabet (x, y, z) are usually applied to variables
  • letters at the beginning of the alphabet (a, b, c) are usually applied to constants
